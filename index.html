About Me

Hello! I'm Onyike Chukwuemeka U., a passionate and results-driven data analyst with a strong background in business and information science. With a Bachelor's Degree in Library and Information Science, a Master’s in Business Analytics, and certifications in Business Intelligence Fundamentals and Lean Six Sigma White Belt, I possess the technical and analytical skills to derive actionable insights from complex datasets.

Currently serving as Sales Manager at Shoe Planet Multi-Concepts Ltd., I blend my expertise in sales management with my data analysis capabilities to drive business growth and optimize performance. I have experience handling diverse datasets, including sales and export data, and enjoy transforming raw information into meaningful insights. My skills span data cleaning, visualization, and dashboard creation using tools like Microsoft Excel, Tableau, and Power BI.

Beyond the office, I’m committed to continuous learning and recently completed Cisco’s Data Analytics Essentials course, where I enhanced my skills in analyzing trends and creating dashboards. Fluent in English and Igbo, with a touch of Efik, I bring strong communication abilities and a collaborative mindset to every project.

I'm deeply interested in using data to bridge gaps and solve real-world challenges, especially in business and development contexts. Whether it’s identifying trends, uncovering insights, or delivering compelling visualizations, I’m excited to explore innovative solutions through data.
